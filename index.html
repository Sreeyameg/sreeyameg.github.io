Peer-reviewed work

Springer Nature · Book Chapter · 2024

Experimental Investigations and AI Modelling of Scour Depths for Improved Structural Integrity

Applied ANN-PSO hybrid and SVM machine learning models to predict scour depth around bridge piers under clear water scouring conditions. Dataset compiled from 16 published experimental sources. ANN-PSO outperformed SVM across all statistical metrics.

Springer Published HYDRO 2024, Pune CBIT Seed Grant
ANN-PSO R² 0.65
MAPE 20.25%
Nash-Sutcliffe E 0.708
SVM R² 0.51
B.E. Final Year Project · CBIT · April 2025

Service Life Prediction of RHA Blended Concrete — Comparison with Fly Ash and GGBS

Used Life-365 software to simulate chloride ingress in RHA, FA and GGBS blended concrete across 54 scenarios covering Chennai and Visakhapatnam coastal zones. RHA at 15–20% replacement achieved 90–110% service life improvement over OPC. Turnitin similarity: 11%.

CBIT — Osmania University Life-365 + OriginPro Guide: Dr. K. Jagannadha Rao
